## Why ChecadorCumbres?

ChecadorCumbres is a Laravel-based SaaS application designed to simplify attendance management for organizations of all sizes. Built with modern web technologies, it provides:

- **Automated Tracking**: Employees check in with a simple employee ID lookup
- **Smart Detection**: Automatically flags late arrivals based on configurable time limits
- **Powerful Reporting**: Export attendance data to PDF or Excel with calculated work hours
- **Email Notifications**: Weekly automated reports of tardiness and absences
- **Admin Dashboard**: Comprehensive filtering and search capabilities
- **Configurable Settings**: Customize check-in times, schedules, and notification preferences
