ChecadorCumbres provides a simple and efficient way for employees to register their check-in and check-out times. The system supports both QR code scanning and manual employee number entry.

## Accessing the Check-In System

The check-in system is available at the root URL (`/`) and can be accessed without authentication. This public interface is designed for ease of use at physical check-in stations.

```php
Route::get('/', [HomeController::class, 'showWelcome']);
Route::get('/empleado/{id}/buscar', [HomeController::class, 'buscarEmpleado']);
```

## Check-In Methods

Employees can register their attendance using two methods:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="QR Code Scanning">

      <Step title="Position Your QR Code">
        Hold your employee QR code in front of the scanner. The system uses HTML5 QR code scanning technology for fast recognition.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Wait for Recognition">
        The system will automatically detect and process your QR code within seconds.
      </Step>

      <Step title="View Confirmation">
        Your photo and name will appear on screen along with a confirmation message.

    </Steps>
  </Tab>

    <Steps>

## Understanding Schedule Types

ChecadorCumbres supports two schedule types, each with different check-in/out behaviors:

### Base Schedule (Horario Base)

Employees with base schedules have fixed entry and exit times. The system enforces the following rules:

<div className="grid grid-cols-1 gap-4 my-4">
  <div>
    **Check-In Window** (Before 11:30 AM by default)
    - The system records your entry time
    - Marks you as late if you arrive after the configured time limit (default: 7:35 AM)
    - Automatically calculates tardiness based on the `hora_limite_entrada` configuration
  </div>

  <div>
    **Check-Out Window** (After 11:30 AM by default)
    - The system records your exit time
    - Links it to your morning check-in record
  </div>
</div>

<Warning>
If you attempt to check out before 11:30 AM (the default `hora_limite_salida`), the system will ask for confirmation. If you check out before recording an entry, the system creates a special record with your check-out time only.
</Warning>

### Flexible Schedule (Horario Libre)

Employees with flexible schedules have more freedom in their timing:

- **First Scan**: Records your entry time
- **Second Scan** (within 1 hour): Shows a confirmation prompt
- **Second Scan** (after 1 hour): Automatically records your exit time
- **No tardiness tracking**: The system doesn't mark flexible schedule employees as late

<Note>
The 1-hour threshold prevents accidental double-scans while allowing quick corrections if needed.
</Note>

## Check-In Workflow

Here's what happens when you check in:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Employee Lookup">
    The system searches for your employee record using the endpoint:
    ```php
    GET /empleado/{id}/buscar
    ```
    If not found, you'll see an error message: "Empleado no encontrado" (Employee not found).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Schedule Check">
    The system retrieves your `tipo_horario` (schedule type) to determine which check-in rules to apply.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Existing Record Check">
    The system checks if you already have an attendance record for today by querying:
    ```php
    Asistencia::where('empleado_id', $empleado->id)
        ->whereDate('hora_entrada', today())
        ->orWhereDate('hora_salida', today())
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Entry Registration">
    If this is your first scan of the day, the system creates a new attendance record:
    ```php
    Asistencia::create([
        'empleado_id' => $empleado->id,
        'hora_entrada' => now(),
        'hora_salida' => null,
        'retardo' => $retardo,
    ]);
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Check-Out Workflow

When you scan for the second time (check-out):

<Steps>
  <Step title="Time Validation">
    For base schedules, the system checks if it's after the configured exit time window (default: 11:30 AM).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirmation Prompt (if early)">
    If you're checking out early or within 1 hour of check-in (for flexible schedules), you'll see:
    
    *"Ya has marcado tu entrada. ¿Quieres marcar tu salida?"* (You've already checked in. Do you want to check out?)

    Click **Sí** (Yes) to confirm or **No** to cancel.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Exit Registration">
    The system updates your existing attendance record:
    ```php
    $asistencia->hora_salida = now();
    $asistencia->save();
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Special Cases and Edge Cases

<Accordion title="Checking out before 1 hour (Flexible Schedule)">
  For flexible schedule employees, if you scan again within 60 minutes of check-in, the system shows a confirmation modal. This prevents accidental check-outs due to double-scanning.

  To confirm your early check-out, click "Sí" in the confirmation dialog.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="No entry record but checking out">
  If you forgot to check in but scan during the check-out window (after 11:30 AM), the system behavior depends on your schedule:

  - **Base Schedule**: Creates a record with `hora_entrada = null`, marks you as late (`retardo = true`)
  - **Flexible Schedule**: Creates a record with both `hora_entrada` and `hora_salida` set to the current time
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Already checked in and out">
  If you try to scan a third time after completing both check-in and check-out, you'll see:
  
  *"Ya tienes registrada la entrada y salida para hoy"* (You already have entry and exit recorded for today)

  No additional records are created.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Employee not found">
  If your employee number or QR code isn't recognized, verify:
  - Your employee record exists in the system
  - Your QR code matches your employee ID
  - Your employee number is entered correctly

  Contact your administrator if the issue persists.
</Accordion>

## Understanding Response Messages

| Message | Meaning |
|---------|----------|
| **Entrada registrada correctamente** | Check-in recorded successfully |
| **Salida registrada correctamente** | Check-out recorded successfully |
| **Ya has marcado tu entrada** | You already checked in today |
| **Ya tienes tu entrada y salida marcadas** | Both check-in and check-out are complete |
| **Empleado no encontrado** | Employee record not found |

## Configuration Options

The following settings can be configured by administrators in the system configuration:

- **hora_limite_entrada** (Default: 07:35:00): The time after which check-ins are marked as late
- **hora_limite_salida** (Default: 11:30:00): The time that separates check-in window from check-out window

<Note>
These configurations are stored in the `configuraciones` table and can be modified through the admin preferences panel.
</Note>
